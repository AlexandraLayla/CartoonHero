About Sailor Moon

A ditsey school girl with a hidden secret that she's a leader of the sailor scouts but also the princess of the Moon. Fighting evil by moonlight but winning love by daylight.

  1. Has a cat called Luna who is also her mentor
  2. She is often called meatball head by her boyfriend/prince Mamoru
  3. She matures eventually into a confident, strong woman who embraces her powers as a sailor senshi and one day queen.
